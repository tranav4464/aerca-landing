Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Aerca ("Aerca", "we", "us") builds an autonomous finance-operations system for agencies and service firms. This policy explains what information we collect, why, how we protect it, and the choices you have. We keep it in plain language on purpose.

1. Who this applies to

This policy covers visitors to our website, people who join our waitlist or reserve founding access, and, once the product is live, agencies who connect their accounts to Aerca.

2. Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Waitlist & founding sign-ups: your name, work email, agency name, and the answers you provide (agency type, team size, the problems you face, tools you use, and an estimate of monthly leakage).
  • Founding application & membership: the answers you give in the founding application or free waitlist. No payment is taken when you reserve. If and when you complete the one-time $49 at launch, it is processed by Stripe — we receive confirmation and limited billing details, and never see or store your full card number.
  • Messages: anything you send us through our contact form (your name, email, and message).

Information from connected tools (after launch)

When you connect a service such as QuickBooks, Xero, Harvest, Toggl, or a project tool, you authorise Aerca through that provider's secure OAuth flow. Our access is read-only. We read operational and financial data needed to do our job (for example logged hours, scope, invoices, payments, and balances). We do not store your login credentials, and we do not modify your records.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage & device data: pages viewed, referring source, approximate location (from IP, which we anonymise), browser, and device, via Google Analytics.
  • Marketing attribution: campaign parameters (UTM tags) so we can understand which channels work.
  • Cookies: see Section 8.

3. How we use your information

  • To operate the waitlist and founding programme, and to send your Cash-Flow Teardown.
  • To provide, secure, and improve the product, and to surface the insights Aerca exists to deliver.
  • To process founding payments and issue refunds.
  • To respond to your messages and provide support.
  • To measure and improve our marketing and website.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud and misuse.

4. Legal bases (EU/UK)

Where the GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we rely on: your consent (for example, joining the waitlist or marketing emails); contract (to deliver founding access you reserved); legitimate interests (to run, secure, and improve our business); and legal obligation where required.

5. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your financial data to train third-party AI models. We share data only with service providers who help us run Aerca, under contract and only as needed:

  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • Formspree — delivery of form submissions to us.
  • Google Analytics — website analytics.
  • Vercel — website hosting.
  • Accounting, time-tracking, and project providers you choose to connect.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to enforce our terms, or in connection with a merger or acquisition (you will be notified).

6. Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law. Waitlist and contact records are kept until you ask us to delete them or they are no longer needed. You can request deletion at any time (Section 7).

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent. To exercise any of these, contact us through the form on our homepage. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

8. Cookies & analytics

We use a small number of cookies and similar technologies for essential functionality and for analytics (Google Analytics). You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling analytics cookies does not affect your ability to use the site.

9. International transfers

We and our providers may process data in countries other than your own, including the United States. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

10. Security

We protect information with encryption in transit and at rest, least-privilege access, and read-only connections to your tools. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we work to industry standards and are building toward SOC 2.

11. Children

Aerca is for businesses and is not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. We will post the new version here and update the date above. Material changes will be communicated where appropriate.

13. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach us through the contact form on our homepage and we will reply by email.
