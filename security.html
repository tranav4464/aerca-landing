Data Security and Encryption

At Aerca, we treat your financial data with the same diligence, transparency, and enterprise-grade security as the world's most trusted platforms. This page explains how we encrypt, store, and protect your information, and our absolute accountability in safeguarding your agency's livelihood.

1. End-to-End and At-Rest Encryption

We apply uncompromising cryptographic standards to ensure your data is secure at all times:

  • In Transit: All data transmitted between your browser, our servers, and third-party integrations (like QuickBooks and Xero) is encrypted using TLS 1.3, the latest standard for internet communications.
  • At Rest: All databases, backups, and uploaded files are encrypted at rest using AES-256 bit encryption, widely recognized as the industry standard for securing highly sensitive financial and government information.

2. Strict Access Controls & Authentication

Security starts with who has access. We have implemented rigorous access control mechanisms:

  • Zero-Trust Architecture: We employ a zero-trust model internally. Aerca staff cannot access your raw financial data or uploaded files without explicit, time-bound, and audited technical necessity.
  • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA): We mandate MFA for all administrative access and encourage all Aerca users to enable MFA for their accounts.
  • Role-Based Permissions: You retain complete control over who within your agency can view, edit, or approve financial actions.

3. Secure Third-Party Integrations

Aerca connects to your existing tools (QuickBooks, Harvest, etc.) through their official, authorized APIs. We only request the minimum permissions required (Principle of Least Privilege). Aerca never asks for or stores your direct login credentials for these services; we use secure OAuth tokens that you can revoke at any time from your provider's dashboard.

4. Compliance & Infrastructure

Our platform is built on top-tier cloud infrastructure providers (like AWS and Google Cloud) that maintain strict compliance certifications, including SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and PCI-DSS. We inherit and build upon these robust security frameworks to ensure your data is hosted in highly secure, fault-tolerant environments.

5. Transparency & Breach Accountability

Trust requires total transparency. In the highly unlikely event of a data breach or security incident:

  • Immediate Notification: We commit to notifying affected users without undue delay, providing full details of what occurred, what data may have been exposed, and what steps we are taking.
  • Root Cause Transparency: We will publicly share the root cause of the incident and the specific security enhancements implemented to prevent it from happening again.
  • Accountability: We hold ourselves to the highest standards of accountability. Your data is your agency's lifeblood, and we view its protection as our most critical responsibility.

6. Data Ownership & Portability

Your data belongs to you. We do not sell your financial data to third parties. If you choose to leave Aerca, you can securely export your data, and we will completely purge your records from our active databases and backups within our standard retention cycle (in compliance with legal requirements).

7. Responsible Disclosure (Bug Bounty)

We welcome collaboration with the security community. If you discover a vulnerability in our systems, we ask that you report it to us responsibly at security@aerca.com. We take all reports seriously and will work swiftly to resolve verified issues.
