Data Security and Encryption

At Aerca, we view the protection of your financial data as our most critical responsibility. As an emerging autonomous finance-operations platform currently in active development, our security posture is being engineered from the ground up to meet and exceed enterprise-grade standards. This document outlines the security measures currently in place for our pre-launch phase, our foundational engineering commitments for the upcoming platform, and our uncompromising stance on AI data privacy.

1. Current Pre-Launch Security Posture

While the full Aerca platform is actively being built, we are already enforcing strict security protocols for our waitlist and founding member applications:

  • Data Minimization: We currently only collect the absolute minimum data required to manage the waitlist and process founding reservations.
  • Payment Security: We do not store, process, or transmit credit card data on our own servers. All founding deposits are securely processed via Stripe, a Level 1 PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway, ensuring your payment credentials remain fully encrypted and tokenized.
  • Secure Transmission: All communications between your browser and our landing page infrastructure are secured using modern TLS (Transport Layer Security) protocols, preventing interception or tampering.

2. Future Platform Architecture: End-to-End & At-Rest Encryption

When the Aerca platform officially launches, it will deploy a robust, multi-layered cryptographic framework designed to protect highly sensitive financial telemetry:

  • In Transit: All data flowing between our servers, your browser, and authorized third-party accounting software (e.g., QuickBooks, Xero) will be encrypted using TLS 1.3, the latest cryptographic standard for internet communications.
  • At Rest: All databases, backups, connected API tokens, and uploaded financial artifacts will be encrypted at rest using AES-256 bit encryption, widely recognized as the gold standard for securing classified and highly sensitive financial data.
  • Zero-Trust Internal Access: Aerca is being engineered on a zero-trust internal architecture. This means our engineering and support staff will not have default access to your raw financial data. Any necessary access for troubleshooting must be explicitly authorized, time-bound, and strictly audited.

3. Artificial Intelligence Privacy and Data Siloing

Aerca leverages advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Large Language Models (LLMs) to automate invoicing, categorize transactions, and generate cash-flow forecasts. We recognize that introducing AI into financial workflows requires absolute transparency and rigid data protection boundaries.

  • Zero-Retention and Non-Training Mandate: We are committed to a strict "no training" policy. Your proprietary financial data, client lists, and transaction histories will NEVER be used to train, fine-tune, or improve our foundational AI models, nor those of any third-party AI provider.
  • Ephemeral Processing: When our AI models process your data to generate a forecast or draft an invoice, the data is processed ephemerally. The AI acts as an isolated reasoning engine, analyzing the data strictly in memory for the duration of the task, without retaining the underlying financial context after the output is generated.
  • Tenant Isolation: Your data remains strictly partitioned. The AI context generated for your agency is logically isolated and cannot leak into or influence the AI outputs generated for any other Aerca user.

4. Secure Third-Party Integrations (OAuth)

To function as an autonomous financial operator, Aerca will connect to your existing toolstack. These connections are being designed around the Principle of Least Privilege:

  • We will solely utilize official, secure OAuth APIs provided by your software vendors.
  • Aerca will never ask for, nor store, your direct username or password for third-party services.
  • We request only the specific permissions necessary to perform authorized tasks, and you maintain the ability to instantly revoke Aerca's access tokens directly from your provider's dashboard at any time.

5. Transparency, Accountability, and Incident Response

Trust in financial technology is sustained through unwavering transparency and accountability. As we build and scale Aerca, we are implementing rigorous incident response protocols:

  • Proactive Auditing: Prior to our public launch, the Aerca platform will undergo comprehensive penetration testing and security auditing by independent third-party cybersecurity firms.
  • Breach Accountability: In the highly unlikely event of a data breach or security incident, we commit to immediate, transparent notification to all affected users without undue delay.
  • Root Cause Analysis: We believe in public accountability. Any security incident will be followed by a transparent root-cause analysis detailing the vectors involved, what data was potentially exposed, and the concrete engineering steps implemented to neutralize the threat permanently.

6. Data Ownership, Portability, and Right to Erasure

Your financial data belongs exclusively to you. Aerca serves merely as a secure custodian and processor of that data to facilitate your agency's operations. We will never sell your data to third-party brokers or advertisers. Furthermore, should you decide to leave the Aerca platform, we guarantee the ability to securely export your data, followed by the complete and permanent cryptographic erasure of your records from our active databases and backups.

7. Responsible Disclosure

We welcome collaboration with the global cybersecurity community. If you discover a vulnerability in our pre-launch infrastructure or waitlist systems, we ask that you report it to us responsibly via the contact form on our homepage. We evaluate all reports with the highest priority and will work swiftly to resolve verified issues.
